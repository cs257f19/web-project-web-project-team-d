Our Mission: To enable users to look at certain aspects of kickstarter projects and how they relate to one or more other variable(s).

We hope that you can use this tool not only to help improve the quality of your kickstarters and investments, but also to find something unexpected or new that might inspire you to support someone in working towards their dream hobby.

Not sure whrere to begin? Feel free to watch random kickstarters scroll past on the right. Alternatively, feel free to play around with the data comparison tool!
